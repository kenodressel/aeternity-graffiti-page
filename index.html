Graffiti on aeternity

Art. On the Blockchain. Forever.

Open Source. No censorship.

This new creative medium, enables the global community to create a graffiti, built on the decentralised, uncensored, transparent blockchain where the work will remain indefinitely, uncensored and open to all.

Unique Artstyle

We worked with artists to define this unique, neon inspired vectorized art style that all images get converted to. Within the transformation process you can tune a long list of parameters that make your image unique.

Collaboration is key.

This artwork thrives in collaboration between artists. Make the images work together, tell a story or modify an existing piece to your liking.

Everyone can do it

Step 1

In your base aepp open graffiti.aeternity.art and upload any image.

Step 2

The image gets transformed on your conditions, edit the parameters and explore the possibilities.

Step 3

Position your image and bid on the image. If your bid is successful, your image stays in the artwork for ever.
